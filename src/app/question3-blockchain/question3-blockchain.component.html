What is Blockchain Technology?

Blockchain is a decentralized ledger system that provides secure storage and recording of transactions and information. It operates on a network of interconnected nodes, where each node holds a copy of the database and participates in verifying transactions. The transactions are then added to the ledger as blocks, forming a chain, creating a secure and tamper-proof record of data. Blockchain technology has various potential applications, such as financial transactions, supply chain management, and voting systems, offering a secure and efficient solution for the transfer and storage of information and assets.
